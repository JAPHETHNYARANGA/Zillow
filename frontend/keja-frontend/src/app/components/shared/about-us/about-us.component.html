About Keja

Connecting people with their perfect homes since 2020

Our Story

Keja was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make finding and listing properties easier, faster, and more transparent for everyone involved in the real estate process.

What started as a small team of real estate enthusiasts and tech innovators has grown into a trusted platform serving thousands of users across the country.

We believe everyone deserves to find a place they can call home, and we're committed to making that process as seamless as possible.
